Teaching Approach

My Philosophy

"Language is not just grammar and vocabulary — it is a window into another world. My role is to open that window as wide as possible."
— Lyza Rendon

Immersion First

From day one, lessons are conducted as much in German as the student can handle, gradually shifting to near-total immersion.

Cultural Context

Every grammar point is tied to a real cultural context — music, film, literature, or current events — to make abstract rules memorable.

Structured Flexibility

A clear curriculum backbone with room for the student's interests. Whether you love football or philosophy, we'll learn German through it.

Fearless Speaking

Mistakes are treated as data, not failures. A safe, judgment-free environment ensures students speak early and speak often.
